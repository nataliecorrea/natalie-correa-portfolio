Who am I, you ask?

I am Natalie Correa, a born and raised New Yorker currently living in the midwest. I am a current Cartography/GIS Masters student studying at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research interests are centered on exploring personal stories and connections to space by making maps and comics. My research methods center around the use of cognitive mapping approaches like mental mapping and sketch mapping.

You want a beautiful map? Are you interested in participatory map creation? Want to see some quick sketches or in-depth drawings? You've come to the right place. Read all about my background and experience or check out my previous projects.

When not working on research, I am likely crocheting, sketching the world around me, taking pictures, or listening to live music. I can also be found eating ice cream in below freezing temperatures or logging entries into my travel journal.
